Competitive Esports

Competitive Esports as an industry has become very popular in modern times. So much so that esports has become a viable option for scholarships,
giving people opportunites that they never would have had otherwise.

Additionally, tournaments for esports can have very large prize pools, drawing in players from all over to compete in high stakes matches
The most prize money across tournaments in 2024 were:

Dota 2 $22.8M
Counter-Strike $21.6M
Honor of Kings $20.6M
PUBG Mobile $16.5M
Fortnite $12.9M
Spectating Esports

Esports can also bring in large crowds of people to spectate. Despite being a relatively new medium, esports tournaments can bring in
millions of viewers at a time. And they are only growing more popular with time.

Here are the games with the most peak viewers in 2024:

League of Legends 6.9M
Mobile Legends: Bang Bang 4.1M
Counter-Strike 1.9M
Valorant 1.7M
Dota 2 1.5M
